Privacy Policy

TruckersPro · Effective July 16, 2026

1. What we collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address, which is used to log you in through Supabase Auth. Beyond that, TruckersPro only stores the business records you enter yourself while using the app: your loads (routes, earnings, TONU, and commission), fuel purchases, weekly expenses, odometer readings, and an optional driver or company name. We do not collect any information you don't type into the app.

2. How it's used

Everything you enter is used solely to provide the app's features — calculating your weekly profit and keeping your data in sync across your devices. TruckersPro contains no advertising and no analytics SDKs. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, for any purpose.

3. Where it's stored

TruckersPro is offline-first: your data lives on your device first, so the app works even without signal. When you have a connection, it's synced to Supabase, a hosted cloud database, so your records are backed up and available on your other devices. That transfer is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS, and row-level security on the database ensures your data can only ever be read or written by your own account.

4. Data retention & deletion

Your data is kept for as long as your account exists. If you'd like your account and all associated data permanently deleted, email fatihatak1907@gmail.com from the email address on your account and we will delete it.

5. Children

TruckersPro is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

6. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL.
